Project Management

How PM applies in Software Development

While some may consider planning in software development a quick task, a pre-start option, I believe the phases of SDLC and those of Project Management are fundamentally intertwined. A well-executed SDLC is a project plan — it is iterative, goal-driven, and sensitive to both scope and resource constraints. Having led projects across highly regulated industries, I understand how to map SDLC phases to real-world milestones: Requirements to stakeholder alignment, Development to team execution, Testing to risk mitigation, Deployment to delivery, and Maintenance to long-term value. It is not just about writing the code — it is about managing people, process, and progress, all moving together.

Foundation

What all this means to me

My career has involved providing support to meet evolving needs of the business and the users responsible to achieve that objective.. I have a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am proficient in Python, Django, Node.js, Express, and React. I have also worked with various databases, including PostgreSQL and MongoDB. My experience with GitHub and Heroku has allowed me to manage and deploy projects effectively. My experience has given me a deep familiarity with what it takes to succeed, and that is the binding of theory, practice and principle. No matter where I am in the lifecycle of the business I am involved in, my experience allows me a full perspective and appreciation for efficiency and accuracy. Success is achieved with the well maintained, meticulously planned and continued push for excellence from every aspect of a business, there are no shortcuts, no easier way.
